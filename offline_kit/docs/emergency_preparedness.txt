EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS GUIDE
==============================

Power Outage:
1. Keep flashlights and charged power banks ready
2. Keep 72-hour supply of water (3 liters per person per day)
3. Keep non-perishable food for at least 3 days
4. Keep cash — ATMs may not work

Earthquake:
1. DROP to hands and knees
2. COVER head and neck under sturdy table or against interior wall
3. HOLD ON until shaking stops
4. Stay away from windows and exterior walls
5. After shaking — check for injuries, gas leaks, fire

Fire:
1. Alert everyone — shout Fire!
2. Call 101 (fire) or 112 (emergency)
3. Evacuate immediately — do not use elevator
4. Close doors behind you to slow fire spread
5. If smoke — crawl low where air is cleaner
6. Never re-enter burning building

Flood:
1. Move to higher ground immediately
2. Do not walk or drive in moving water
3. Disconnect electrical appliances
4. Listen to emergency broadcasts

72-Hour Emergency Kit Checklist:
- Water: 3 liters per person per day
- Food: non-perishable items
- First aid kit
- Flashlight + extra batteries
- Phone charger + power bank
- Cash in small bills
- Copies of important documents
- Medications (7-day supply)
- Whistle, dust masks, manual can opener
