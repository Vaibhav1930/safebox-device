FIRST AID — QUICK REFERENCE
=============================

CPR (Adult):
1. Check scene safety, tap shoulders, shout "Are you okay?"
2. Call emergency services (112 in India)
3. 30 chest compressions — push hard 2 inches deep, 100-120 per minute
4. 2 rescue breaths — tilt head, lift chin, cover mouth, breathe in
5. Repeat until help arrives

Choking:
1. Ask "Are you choking?"
2. 5 back blows between shoulder blades
3. 5 abdominal thrusts (Heimlich) — hands above navel, thrust inward+upward
4. Alternate until object dislodges or person loses consciousness

Bleeding:
1. Apply firm direct pressure with clean cloth
2. Elevate injured limb above heart if possible
3. Do not remove cloth — add more on top
4. Seek medical help if bleeding does not stop in 10 minutes

Burns:
1. Cool with running water for 10–20 minutes — do NOT use ice
2. Cover with clean non-stick bandage
3. Do NOT pop blisters
4. Seek medical help for large burns

Fever:
1. Give paracetamol (Crocin/Tylenol) as directed
2. Keep cool — remove extra clothing
3. Give fluids — water, ORS
4. Seek doctor if fever above 103F or lasts more than 3 days

Emergency Numbers India:
- Police: 100
- Ambulance: 108
- Fire: 101
- All emergencies: 112
- Poison control: 1800-116-117
