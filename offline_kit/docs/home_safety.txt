HOME SAFETY GUIDE
==================

Gas Leak:
1. Do NOT turn on or off any switches or lights
2. Do NOT use phone inside the building
3. Open all windows and doors
4. Leave the building immediately
5. Call gas company from outside

Water Shutoff:
1. Main water valve is usually near water meter
2. Turn clockwise to shut off
3. For leaks — shut off immediately and call plumber

Electrical Safety:
1. Never touch outlets or switches with wet hands
2. Do not overload power strips
3. If circuit breaker trips — unplug devices before resetting

Security:
1. Lock all doors and windows before sleeping or leaving
2. Do not open door to strangers
3. Install smoke detectors on every floor
4. Test smoke detectors monthly

Child Safety:
1. Cover electrical outlets with safety caps
2. Keep medicines locked and out of reach
3. Keep small objects away from children under 3
4. Never leave children unattended near water
