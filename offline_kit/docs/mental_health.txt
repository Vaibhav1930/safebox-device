MENTAL HEALTH — CRISIS SUPPORT
================================

Crisis Helplines India:
- iCall: 9152987821
- Vandrevala Foundation: 1860-2662-345 (24/7)
- NIMHANS: 080-46110007

Signs of mental health crisis:
- Talking about wanting to die or hurt themselves
- Extreme mood changes, hopelessness
- Talking about being a burden to others

What to do:
1. Stay calm — do not panic
2. Listen without judgment
3. Ask directly: Are you thinking about suicide?
4. Do not leave them alone
5. Call crisis helpline or take to nearest hospital

Anxiety — immediate relief:
1. 4-7-8 breathing: inhale 4 counts, hold 7, exhale 8
2. Grounding: name 5 things you see, 4 you hear, 3 you can touch
3. Cold water on face or wrists

Depression — daily support:
1. Maintain routine — wake and sleep at same time
2. Small physical activity — even 10 minute walk helps
3. Stay connected — talk to one person daily
4. Seek professional help — it is a medical condition, not weakness
