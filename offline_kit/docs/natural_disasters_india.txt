NATURAL DISASTERS — INDIA SPECIFIC
=====================================

Cyclone:
1. Monitor IMD alerts: mausam.imd.gov.in
2. Move away from coast to designated shelter
3. Secure loose outdoor items
4. Fill water containers before storm hits
5. Stay indoors during storm — away from windows

Heat Wave:
1. Stay indoors during peak heat (12pm to 4pm)
2. Drink water even if not thirsty — 2 to 3 liters per day
3. Wear light, loose, light-colored clothing
4. Never leave children or pets in parked cars
5. Signs of heat stroke: high temp, no sweating, confusion — call 112

Landslide:
1. Watch for warning signs: cracks in ground, tilting trees
2. Move to higher stable ground immediately
3. Do not return until authorities declare area safe

Thunderstorm Lightning:
1. Get indoors immediately
2. Unplug electrical appliances
3. If outdoors: crouch low, feet together
4. Stay away from tall trees, metal fences, water
